Our Technology

At VegDoc, we utilize advanced machine learning algorithms and artificial intelligence to provide accurate and effective pesticide recommendations. Our technology takes into account various factors such as crop type, pest type, to generate customized recommendations that are tailored to your specific needs. With our state-of-the-art technology, we can help you achieve healthier crops, higher yields, and ultimately, greater profit

CRITERIA

When recommending the right pesticide for each disease, we take into consideration various factors such as the type of crop, the specific disease that is affecting the crop, the stage of the disease, and the severity of the infestation. Our system uses machine learning algorithms to analyze all of these factors and provide the best recommendation for each case. Additionally, we also take into account any environmental or health concerns, and strive to recommend the safest and most effective solution for each situation.
